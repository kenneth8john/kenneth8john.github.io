Wow Philippines!

Cebu

Cebu is a perfect destination for tourists. There are many forest reserves, mountains and hills, that are perfect for those who love forest trekking.

Siargao

Siargao is best known for its reliable surfing conditions all year round, which have earned it the title of the "Surfing Capital of the Philippines."

Siquijor

Siquijor is a mysterious other-world of witchcraft. True, this tiny island province is famous for its mountain-dwelling mangkukulam.

Bohol

The home of the famous Chocolate Hills, Bohol is one of the most visited destinations in the Central Visayas region of the Philippines.

Palawan

Palawan is a world-renowned destination for its breathtaking land and seascapes, offering unparalleled cultural, and adventure experiences.

Subic

Subic Bay is a favorite touristic destination for Filipino and foreign tourists. The area hosts a beautiful body of deep waters surrounded by mountain.

Cagayan Valley

Cagayan not to be confused with the Philippines city of Cagayan de Oro, is a province in the Philippines that recently gotten a lot of attention.

La Union

Known as surfing capital of the North, La Union offers a vast array of things to do and places to go. If you want to be one with the waves, go surfing in ELYU.

Baler

Baler, the capital of the province of Aurora is a known surfing spot in the country.
