BELOW YOU'LL FIND SOME OF MY MOST FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS INCLUDING THE PERSONAL STUFF!

If you'd like to know more, make sure you follow my adventures on my social media channels.

Do you travel alone?

Solo travel is my favorite way to travel. it’s the best way to meet new people along the way, and totally immerse yourself in another culture. Having said that, I’m a social creature and love to jump on adventures with other people. It really depends on where I’m going and what I’ll be doing, but I try to strike a healthy balance between the two.

What is your travel philosophy?

I believe in travel that is sustainable, community-oriented and wholehearted. I believe in immersing myself in other cultures, living with local people and trying to understand the way they live and work. For me, this is the kind of travel that helps me make a small difference, challenges me in positive ways and helps me grow as a human being.

How do you choose a destination?

If you can dream it, you can do it. One of my guiding principles in everything I do, and it applies to travel, too. I have a running list in my head of some of the world’s most remote, spectacular and inaccessible destinations. In fact, I take it on as a challenge to find a way to visit them, because I know that where there is a will, there is a way.

What gear do you use for photos & videos?

I'm a big believer in minimalism, which is why my gear is pretty simple and light. I travel with one camera and one lens, an action cam and my drone.

What outdoor gear do you use?

As a minimalist at heart, I prefer to get fewer, but higher quality items that accompany me on all my travels. I always travel with my camping gear, just in case I ever feel like pitching my tent and spending the night in the outdoors.

What is your ultimate goal in your life?

My ultimate goal in life is to continue learning and growing in line with my inner truth, bettering the world around me in the process.
